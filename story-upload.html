Tips for Great Bedtime Stories

  • Keep your story between 5-10 minutes when read aloud (about 500-1000 words).
  • Include calming elements like gentle animals, stars, or peaceful settings.
  • Create characters that children can relate to and care about.
  • End with a peaceful resolution that encourages sleep and sweet dreams.
  • Consider adding a gentle moral or lesson that children can reflect on.
